TECHNOLOGY SHOULD
NOT BE A WEAPON.

AEAH is dedicated to the ethical oversight of emerging technology. We stand as a barrier between innovation and harm, ensuring that progress never comes at the cost of human safety.

User Protection

We identify and mitigate digital threats that target individuals, focusing on psychological and physical safety in a connected world.

Ethical Engineering

Advocating for "Safety by Design." We work with developers to ensure technology is built with human-first guardrails from day one.

Public Vigilance

Exposing the misuse of tech. When platforms or tools are used to exploit or harm, AEAH sounds the alarm.

"We don't want people hurting themselves... and we won't let tech be used to hurt others."
